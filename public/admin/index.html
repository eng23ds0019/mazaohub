Where do articles appear on the website?

Each article you create or edit appears on the public Field Notes / Blog page at yoursite.com/#/news. Clicking an article title opens it at /#/news/[slug]. Articles are NOT part of the homepage scrolling sections — those are edited under Edit Page Sections.

Understanding Categories

Categories allow you to group your blog articles together (e.g. "Soil Advisory" or "Organic Advisory"). When creating or editing articles, you can assign them to a category. Articles without a category will appear under "Uncategorized".

  2. Paste this URL into your Articles (for Featured Images) or Page Sections editor to display the media.
